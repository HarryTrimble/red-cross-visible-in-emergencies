Cumbria floods 2019

If you’ve been affected by the Cumbria floods free and confidential support is available:

Temporary accommodation
Local councils can provide shelter overnight and temporary housing to people who've had to evacuate their homes.
Financial assistane
Red Cross can provide £40 a day for living costs like food, transport or anything else you need to get by day-to-day.
Insurance advice
Citizens Advice can provide impartial insurance advice to help you navigate making a claim with an insurance company.
Grants to repair your home and replace lost possessions
Local councils and central government can provide financial grants to help pay to repair your home and replace lost possessions.
Emotional support
NHS provides professional emotional support for people affected by the floods.

Support is available from different organisations like Cumbria County Council, NHS and charities like the Red Cross.
