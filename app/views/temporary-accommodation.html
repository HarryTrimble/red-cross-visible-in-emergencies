Staying with family and friends

If you can, go to family or friends for the night while we support you with your next steps.

If you have don't have somewhere to stay tonight, speak to Red Cross who can assist you finding somewhere.





Council provided accommodation

{% include "includes/local-authority-name.html" %} can provide overnight shelter and temporary accommodation to people who have had to evacuate their homes.

Temporary acommendation includes:

  • Beds at local help centre
  • Rooms at local hotels
  • Rental properties in and around Derry
  • Rental properties in neighbouring areas

Contact to Wellport about temporary accommodation

Speak to the Red Cross who can assist contacting the Wellport Council.





Insurance provided accommodation

If you have home insurance, your insurance company should pay for your accommodation while your home is being repaired.

Speak to the Red Cross who can assist contacting your insurance company.
